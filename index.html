Insights from the Data Collected

Poverty

As the poverty rate (%) increases, we can see an increase in all 3 attributes of obesity and smoking rates, and also the rate in which healthcare is lacking.

This shows that higher rates of poverty can negatively impact the health of residents within the state.


Age

We can see that the median age of people surveyed is around 36-40 years.

Further, we can see that states with a higher median age, generally have lower rates of lacking healthcare, but also generally have higher rates of smokers. The trend between obesity rates and age are a little less obvious


Household Income

Conversely to the poverty parameter, we can see a positive impact on the health of residents within the state as household income increases, exhibited by lower rates of obesity, smoking and a lack of healthcare
